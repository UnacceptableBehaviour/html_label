Recipe Title

Crab Cake w/ Prawns, Mango Salsa and Mixed Salad

Recipe Description

Lightly seasoned British crab patties, seared until golden brown and topped with whole prawns and a spicy mango salsa. Served w/ a celreiac, squash and pear salad dressed with rock salt and lemon.

Suitable for pescatarians.
