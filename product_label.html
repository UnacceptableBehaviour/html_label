Product Title

Apple Chilli Humous & Fennel Jam Wrap

Product Description

Apple chilli humous, topped with cucumber mint & coriander, carrots, charred onions, crispy onion & red pepper, all brought to life with some spring onion & pickled onions and topped with a gorgeous orange and fennel jelly wrapped in a wholemeal tortilla.

Suitable for vegans.
