How To: Make a Giant Sausage Roll


Roll the pastry out 1m x 22cm

Place sausage meat over the pastry

Egg was & fold pastry over

Seal the seam w/ a fork


Fold it over so its all and even width

Eggwash

& use a fork to finish the seal


Slice into desired size rolls

Place on baking tray lined w/ silpat
